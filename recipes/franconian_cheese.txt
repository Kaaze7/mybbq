Franconian cheese "with music"

Ingredients:

- 4 lumps of Franconian sour milk cheese
- 1 large yellow onion
- 1 ts caraway seeds
- 4 ts sunflower oil
- 2 ts white vinegar
- salt
- pepper

Preparation:

Mix oil, vinegar, salt, pepper, caraway seeds and sliced onion (dressing = "music").
Cut cheese into small pieces and add to dressing.
