Franconian Roasted Onions

Ingredients:

- 8 large red onions
- 8 slices of bacon
- 0.2 l red wine
- 0.2 l wine vinegar
- salt
- pepper

Preparation:

Mix 0.5 l water, wine and vinegar with 1 ts salt, and let boil.
Add peeled onions and let cook for 10 minutes.
Apply pepper and salt to onions and wrap with bacon slices.
Roast on barbecue until bacon starts curling up.
